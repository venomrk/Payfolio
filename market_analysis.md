# Payfolio - Market Analysis & Strategy

## 1. Market Problem
**The Core Issue:** Freelancers and agencies struggle with disjointed payment and portfolio systems.
- **Fragmented Tools:** Creators use one tool for invoices (Stripe/PayPal), another for portfolios (Carrd/Webflow), and another for client communication.
- **Trust Deficit:** Clients are hesitant to pay upfront, and freelancers fear non-payment. Escrow services are expensive and complex.
- **High Fees:** Platforms like Upwork/Fiverr take 20% cuts.

**The Solution:** Payfolio is a "Storefront for Services" that combines a professional portfolio with integrated payments, contracts, and milestone trackingâ€”charging 0% platform fees on the first $1k/mo.

## 2. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
### Primary ICP
*   **Segment:** Freelance Designers, Developers, and Marketers.
*   **Persona:** "Solopreneur Sam" - 3-5 years exp, earns $5k-$15k/mo, tired of Upwork fees.
*   **Pain Point:** losing 20% of revenue to marketplaces; wants a branded, professional checkout experience.
*   **Region:** Global (Stripe supported countries).

### Secondary ICP
*   **Segment:** Small Agencies (2-10 people).
*   **Persona:** Agency owners needing a client portal for billing and deliverables.

## 3. Revenue Model
**Freemium SaaS**
*   **Free Forever:** 1 Portfolio Page, 3% Transaction Fee, Basic Invoicing.
*   **Pro ($29/month):** Custom Domain, 0% Transaction Fees (standard Stripe fees apply), Contracts, Recurring Billing.
*   **Agency ($99/month):** White-label (Client Portal), Team Members, API Access.

Target: **$83,333 MRR**
